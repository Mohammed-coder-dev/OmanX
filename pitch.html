Vision

A single, ministry-grade source of truth for Omani scholarship onboarding abroad.

OmanX reduces confusion, compliance risk, and institutional burden by delivering structured, source-constrained guidance for sponsored students. It is designed for auditability, regulatory alignment, and rapid national rollout.

MVP scope

Intentionally minimal to validate ministry trust and operational value.

  • Three canonical onboarding flows (arrival, compliance, academic/living setup).
  • Checklist-driven guidance with explicit completion states and source categories.
  • Constrained assistant with structured responses and mandatory escalation for high-risk topics.
  • No user accounts, no stored chat history, no sensitive personal data collection.

Expansion roadmap

Scalable path from MoHE to embassies, universities, and additional countries.

  1. MoHE approval and policy governance workflows.
  2. Embassy and university integrations for official escalation paths.
  3. Multi-country coverage with localized compliance modules.

Governance model

Transparent, auditable, and compatible with ministry oversight.

Policy control

MoHE-approved content in Official Mode, community guidance explicitly labeled and separable.

Compliance controls

Rate limiting, secure logging without raw prompts, and stateless backend by design.

Audit readiness

Structured responses with explicit source categories and defined escalation pathways.
